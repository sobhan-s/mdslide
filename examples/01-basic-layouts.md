# mdslide Basic Layouts

### A guide to standard layouts and structural syntax

---

## Slide Separation & Structure

By default, slides are separated by three dashes `---` on an empty line.

You can manually trigger specific slide layouts or let the compiler auto-detect them based on the elements on the slide.
